About Me

I’m Khashayar -- Principal AI Engineer at Inframatic Engineering, where I design and deploy adaptive AI systems for automated compliance in the UK. My focus is on learning agents that operate and adapt in real time inside dynamic, non-stationary environments -- translating advanced ideas in online learning, reinforcement learning, and agent architectures into scalable, production-ready systems.

Day-to-day, I work at the seam where research meets engineering: shaping the architecture of multi-agent pipelines, owning the path from prototype to production, and making sure the systems we ship hold up under real-world load. I care about reliability and observability as much as model quality -- the work that turns a clever idea into something a customer can depend on.

My technical interests sit across agentic AI, continual learning, MCP and multi-agent systems, MLOps, and the kind of MLE work that turns a paper into something dependable. I’ve been writing Python for over a decade, and I’m most at home where theory meets what actually ships.
