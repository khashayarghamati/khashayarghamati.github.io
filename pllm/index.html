Towards AI-Powered Applications: The Development of a Personalised LLM for HRI and HCI

About the Paper

This paper presents the development of a Personalised Large Language Model (PLLM) for Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). The proposed framework integrates domain-specific data to personalize large language models, improving adaptability and user-centric AI applications.

Abstract

This work proposes a Personalised Large Language Model (PLLM) agent designed to enhance adaptability in HRI and HCI applications. Traditional LLMs struggle with dynamic adaptation to user-specific contexts. We introduce a novel approach to personalisation, integrating the NeuroSense EEG dataset to enable real-time emotional state interpretation.
